Wheelchair Accessibility for Transit Across Seattle

This project visualizes neighborhood-level accessibility of transit(subway, buses, etc.) for mobility-limited residents across Seattle census tracts. It displays how many transit centers the average resident in these census tracts can reach and the difficulty of getting there.

Built as an extension of the OpenSidewalks project at the UW Taskar Center, this map highlights structural inequities in urban mobility.

Gray areas indicate insufficient or unavailable data.
