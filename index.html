Wheelchair Accessibility Across Seattle

This project visualizes neighborhood-level accessibility for mobility-limited residents across Seattle census tracts.

Built as an extension of the OpenSidewalks project at the UW Taskar Center, this map integrates accessibility sidewalk metrics with geospatial analysis to highlight structural inequities in urban mobility.

Gray areas indicate insufficient or unavailable data.
